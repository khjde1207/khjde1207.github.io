Ungayisebenzisa kanjani (kumele ifundwe):

Lolu hlelo lokusebenza lunemisebenzi eminingi, ngakho-ke ngincoma ukuthi uyifunde ngakunye.

1. Ungawahlola kanjani amafayela:
Sicela uchofoze Faka ifayela kudokhumenti yami ukuze ungeze i-TXT.
Thinta ifayela le-TXT bese ucindezela inkinobho evulekile ngezansi ukuze ubuke ifayela.


2. Ungayifihla Kanjani Izikhangiso Zomhleli / Izinkinobho Zokuhambisa Ezingaphansi:
Thepha kabili enkabeni yesikrini.
Njengoba ushintshela esikrinini esigcwele, izikhangiso nezinkinobho ziyanyamalala.


3. Ukushintsha indawo yokusebenza kwenkinobho:
Ungayishintsha ngaphansi kwezilungiselelo - amasethingi endawo yokuthinta.
Ungasetha eminye imisebenzi, kufaka phakathi isikrini esigcwele, ukunyakaza kwekhasi, ukulungiswa kokukhanya, nokulawulwa kwevolumu.
I-Bluetooth Remote Control nayo iyasekelwa, ngakho-ke ungalala phansi uyibuke kahle.


4. Ukushintsha usayizi wefonti noma yefonti:
Ungashintsha usayizi wefonti noma yefonti ngaphansi kwezilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo zefonti.
Ungasebenzisa ifonti ngokucindezela inkinobho yokulanda ukuthola inqwaba yamafonti wamahhala.


5. Ungayisebenzisa kanjani ukuguqulwa kombhalo wesithombe:
Ungakhipha umbhalo kusuka esithombeni esicindezelwe. Lokhu kubizwa ngokuthi umsebenzi we-OCR.
Ijenali Yami - Chofoza okuthi Faka ifayela bese wengeza ifayela le-zip.
Ungaguqula ngokukhetha ifayela le-zip bese ukhetha imenyu ye-OCR.
Ikhasi liyadliwa ngesikhathi sokuguqulwa.
I-OCR isebenza nge-AI, ngakho-ke ijubane layo nokunemba kwayo kuhle.


6. I-EPUB, indlela yokuguqula ye-PDF:
Kuyafana nendlela yokuguqulwa kwesithombe.
Kunezinto ezimbalwa okufanele wazi.
I-EPUB noma i-PDF ingahle ibe "ngemfihlo".
Isetshenziselwa ikakhulukazi ukuphula izinhlamvu futhi kubenze babonakale kuphela kumafonti athile.
Kulokhu, kuphuma njengomlingiswa ongemva kokungemthetho, hhayi isiKorea, ngakho-ke awukwazi ukuyifunda kuma-TTS.
Njengendlela yokuxazulula le nkinga, umsebenzi wokukhipha izithombe ungasetshenziswa, bese i-OCR ingajikelezwa ukuze ikhishwe.


7. Ukusetha kanjani ama-TTS:
Izilungiselelo ezihlobene ne-TTS zigxile kuzilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo ze-TTS, izilungiselelo zokuhlunga ze-TTS.
Izilungiselelo ze-TTS zifaka izici ezilula ze-TTS, njengezwi, ijubane, ivolumu, ukuvela kwenkinobho, izinhlamvu eziqeda imisho, kanye nenombolo yemisho efundwa ngesikhathi, kanye namadivayisi anika izici ze-TTS.
Inani lemisho efundwa ngesikhathi umsebenzi owengeziwe ongeziwe ukuze uvumele imigqa eminingi ukuthi ifundwe ngaso leso sikhathi ukuze izwakale ngokubambezeleka okuncane.
Isihlungi se-TTS kungenye yezinto ezinamandla kakhulu zalolu hlelo lokusebenza.
[Izilungiselelo zokuhlunga ze-TTS] zikuvumela ukuthi usethe inani lezinhlamvu ezikhethekile, amagama noma imisho okudingeka yeqe.
Uma ucindezela inkinobho yokulanda, ungalanda izinqwaba zokuhlunga engikukhiqizile.
Ungangeza futhi ngokwakho uma uyidinga.
[Umsebenzi wokuvala okuzenzakalelayo] ungasetha isikhathi sokuvala esizenzakalelayo ngokuthinta isithonjana esenziwe ngenyanga ngakwesokunxele inkinobho yokumisa lapho udlala ama-TTS.


8. Ukuchazwa kwe-Backup kanye namakhono wokutakula:
Ukusetha - Isipele nokululama kuyisici esivumela amadivayisi amaningi ukuthi asebenzise uhlelo lokusebenza endaweni efanayo.
Ungangena ngemvume kwidrayivu yokubuyiselwa kwesipele.
Uma kubukwa kusuka kumadivayisi amaningi, njengama-PC, amafoni, namaphakethe, ungasebenzisa isipele sendawo ukuvumelanisa indawo yakho yokubuka.
Khetha idivaysi ofuna ukuyisebenzisa i-AutoSynchronize bese uvula isici se-autosynchronization ukuze uhambise ngokuzenzakalelayo endaweni yokugcina lapho usebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza noma ubuye ekhaya uphinde ubuye.

9. Ungayisebenzisa kanjani ukuguqulwa kombhalo we-batch:
1. [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo - Inkinobho Yokuhlela Eningi]
2. [Izilungiselelo - Hlela Umbhalo Ngokuhlangene]
Ungasebenzisa ukuguqulwa kwe-batch ngezindlela ezimbili.
Uma kwenzeka inombolo 1, isetshenziselwa ukulalela ama-TTS nokusula noma ukuguqula umusho komunye umlingiswa uma eminye imisho noma amagama ayacasula.
Uma kwenzeka inombolo 2, ungakhipha umbhalo nge-OCR bese ukhetha ifayela lombhalo kusuka kudokhumenti ukwenza ukulungiswa kwe-batch.
Ungasebenzisa futhi inkinobho yokulanda ukulanda futhi usebenzise amaphutha wokuqashelwa kaningi ngokulungiswa kwe-batch kombhalo.
Ukulungiswa kwe-Bulk - Ukulanda kuzokhombisa kuphela uhlu uma izilungiselelo zolimi ziseKorea.
Lokhu kungenxa yokuthi abathuthukisi abakhulumi ezinye izilimi.
Uma ubika usebenzisa umsebenzi wombiko, ngizongeza ulimi lwezwe.

10. Uma kunomusho wokuhlaselwa lapho ulalele ama-TTS, usebenze:
Uma kungakhathalekile ukuthi okwangempela kushintshiwe, sicela ulishintshele ku-Confect Modification] umsebenzi.
Ngemuva koshintsho, ifayela lesipele lizosala umtapo wezincwadi wami, ngakho-ke uma ulishintsha ngephutha, ungaphinde uthole futhi.
Uma ufuna ukususa okwangempela kuphela kusuka kuma-TTS ngaphandle kokuthinta, sebenzisa isihlungi se-TTS.

11. Ikhasi elihlobene Ikhasi elihlobene:
Uma usebenzisa imisebenzi ekhethekile efana nokuhlolwa kwemisho ye-TTS, ukulungiswa kwemisho ye-batch, ukuhlelwa kabusha kwemigqa, nokuhumusha, kuzodliwa amakhasi, kepha njengoba kungama-10 kuye kwangama-50, ngeke uzizwe kudliwe.
Amakhasi amaningi kungenzeka asetshenziselwe ukuzizwa lapho ekhipha umbhalo ovela ku-OCR, ePub, ne-PDF.
Lapho sikhipha umbhalo, asikhiphi nje esinye ngesandla, kodwa futhi sihlele imisho ngokwenza izinkinga eziningi ezivela ngemuva kokukhishwa kwamaphethini.
Kuyingxenye bekunzima ngempela ukuyisebenzisa, ngakho-ke ngicela uyizame.


12. Incazelo Yokukhangisa:
Ngingunjiniyela nobabili nomsebenzisi.
Sengeze izikhangiso kuze kufike lapho zingaphazamisi ukusetshenziswa ngangokunokwenzeka.
Izikhangiso zifaka izikhangiso ezivikeleke ekhasini nezikhangiso zokususa izikhangiso.
Ikhasi lingabamba amakhasi afinyelela kuma-60,000.
Lapho ususa izikhangiso, ungasebenzisa ikhasi ukususa izikhangiso isikhathi esithile. (Ngaphandle kwesikhathi sokulala, kucishe kube usuku.)
Kulabo abangamakhasi aqala ukusetshenziswa, ungasusa umkhangiso oseceleni wesibukeli ngokuphuza amakhasi angama-2000.
Uma usebenzisa amakhasi angama-8000, zonke izikhangiso zikhutshaziwe ngaphandle kwezikhangiso ezi-30 zesibili zokutholwa kwekhasi nemikhangiso yendabuko phakathi kwezilungiselelo.
Izikhangiso zekhasi le-8000 zengezwe zalabo abakhathazwa yizikhangiso ezimfushane ezivela ngesikhathi senani elikhulu lokuguqulwa.


13. Isici Sencazelo Yendlebe (Umlando Ukusebenzelana Nokusebenzelana):
Ixhasa umsebenzi wokubuka kwezindlebe.
Isibonelo, ake sithi sinedokhumenti enesihloko esithi (1 kuya ku-50), futhi sinombhalo omusha obizwa nge (1 kuye ku-100).
Kulokhu, sekuvele kudingekile ukweqa isikhundla esikhulu.
Uma ukhetha ifayela lombhalo kumtapo wezincwadi wami, imenyu yokubuka izindlebe iyavela.
Uma ukhetha le menyu bese ukhetha (1 kuye ku-50) emlandweni, kuzokweqa isikhundla esibukwe ngaphambilini.
Uma usunaso isikhathi emuva kwesikhathi, sicela ususe (1-50) emlandweni.
Emlandweni, inani eliphelele lezincwadi ezifundwe libalwa.
Uma idokhumenti efanayo ihlala, imininingwane engalungile ingakhonjiswa.


14. I-bookmark / inothi / incazelo yenkanyezi:
Uhlelo lokusebenza lisekela amanothi ne-bookmark / stiring.
Uma uvula umsebenzi we-bookmark kuzilungiselelo zebhandiki, ungangeza ngokucindezela nokubamba umbhalo.
Isici samanothi sikuvumela ukuthi ukhethe futhi ufake ifayela uma usuyivulile okungenani kanye.
Uma uvula ifayela okungenani kanye, inkanyezi ivela phezulu kokukhethwa kwefayela.
Amanothi nama-Horoscopes angasetshenziselwa ukuqopha ukuhlolwa kwefayela uqobo.
Amabhukhimakhi angasetshenziselwa ukuqopha okuqukethwe okubalulekile.



14. Incazelo yomhloli wemisho ye-TTS:
Lapho ngibheka imibhalo eminingi, ngaqala ukubona iphethini yemibhalo ebhalwe kahle futhi engabhalwanga.
Ngithole iphethini ephuma njalo emibhalweni emibi.
Ngihlele le ndlela futhi ngiyikhiqize ngokukala iphethini ngayinye.
Sikuqambe igama elithi [Izamina] ekuqaleni.
Kodwa-ke, lo msebenzi wenziwa ngesahlulelo esinezinhloso enkulu, ngakho-ke ngakushintsha ku-TTS Checker.
Lesi sici sisekela iKorea kuphela.
Angazi ulimi lwamanye amazwe, ngakho-ke angikwazi ukuhlela amaphethini ami ukuthi ngabe kuhle noma kubi.
Uma kunephethini elinjengale kwamanye amazwe, sicela usithumele kithi nge-imeyili.
Lapho ungaxhasa izilimi eziningi zezwe lonke, sizohambisa umsebenzi kuzilungiselelo.
Kusekhona indawo eyodwa eshiywe kuzilungiselelo.


15. Ukuchazwa kokusebenza kwe-Dog Administration:

16. Incazelo yomsebenzi wokuhumusha:
Ukusebenza kwendawo ukuhumusha / ukuguqulwa kwewebhu ukuhumusha kuyasekelwa.
Ukuhumusha kwendawo kungumsebenzi ohumusha ku-AI athuthukiswe yi-Google. Amagama alula angahunyushwa kahle, kepha kubonakala sengathi anokungahambi kahle okuningi ekuhumusheni umusho.
