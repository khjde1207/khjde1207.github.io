Ungayisebenzisa kanjani (kumele ifundwe):

Lolu hlelo lokusebenza lunezici eziningi, ngakho-ke ngincoma ukuyifunda.

1. Ungawahlola kanjani amafayela:
Sicela uchofoze Faka ifayela kulabhulali yami ukuze ungeze i-TXT.
Ungabuka ifayela ngokuthinta ifayela le-TXT bese ucindezela inkinobho evulekile ngezansi.


2. Ungazifihla kanjani izikhangiso zokubuka / izinkinobho zokuhambisa ezingezansi:
Thepha kabili enkabeni yesikrini.
Uma ishintsha isikrini esigcwele, izikhangiso nezinkinobho ziyanyamalala.


3. Ungayishintsha kanjani inkinobho nendawo yokusebenza:
Ungayishintsha ku- [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo zendawo yokuthinta].
Ungasetha imisebenzi ehlukahlukene efana nesikrini esigcwele, ukunyakaza kwekhasi, ukulawulwa kokukhanya, nokulawulwa kwevolumu.
Isilawuli kude se-Bluetooth siyasekelwa futhi, ngakho-ke ungayibeka endaweni bese uyibheka kahle ngenkathi ilele phansi.


4. Ungashintsha kanjani usayizi wefonti noma umbhalo:
Ungashintsha usayizi wefonti noma yefonti ku [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo zefonti].
Ungasebenzisa inqwaba yamafonti wamahhala ngokuchofoza inkinobho yokulanda.


5. Ungasisebenzisa kanjani isithombe ekuguqulweni kombhalo kombhalo:
Ungakhipha umbhalo kusuka ezithombeni ezicindezelwe. Lokhu kubizwa ngokuthi umsebenzi we-OCR.
Umtapo Wami - Chofoza u-Faka ifayela bese ufaka ifayela le-zip.
Ungaguqula ngokukhetha ifayela le-zip bese ukhetha imenyu ye-OCR.
Amakhasi adliwa ngesikhathi sokuguqulwa.
I-OCR yenziwa nge-AI, ngakho-ke ijubane layo nokunemba kwawo kuhle impela.


6. Ungaguqula kanjani i-EPUB to PDF:
Kuyafana nendlela yokuguqulwa kwesithombe.
Kunezinto ezimbalwa okufanele uzigcine engqondweni.
Endabeni ye-EPUB noma ye-PDF, kungahle kube khona "kubethelwe".
Indlela evame kakhulu ukwenza izinhlamvu zibe zibunjiwe futhi zibonakale kuphela ngamafonti athile.
Kulokhu, akunakufundwa kusetshenziswa ama-TTS ngoba kubukeka njengesimo somfokazi hhayi isiKorea.
Ukuxazulula le nkinga, ungasebenzisa umsebenzi wokukhishwa kwesithombe bese usebenzisa i-OCR ukuyikhipha.


7. Ukusetha kanjani ama-TTS:
Izilungiselelo ezihlobene ne-TTS zigxile ku- [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo ze-TTS] nezilungiselelo ze-TTS finge].
Izilungiselelo ze-TTS zifaka hhayi kuphela izici ze-TTS ezilula njengezwi, isivinini, ivolumu, ukucaca kwenkinobho, uhlamvu lokugcina umusho, nenombolo yemisho efundwayo ngasikhathi sinye, kodwa futhi namadivayisi ahambisana nomsebenzi we-TTS.
Inani lemisho efundwe ngasikhathi sinye ngumsebenzi ongeziwe ukuvumela ukuthi ufunde imigqa eminingi ngasikhathi sinye ulalele ngokubambezeleka okuncane.
Isihlungi se-TTS ngesinye sezici ezinamandla kakhulu zalolu hlelo lokusebenza.
Kuzilungiselelo ze- [TTS file izilungiselelo], ungabeka izinhlamvu ezahlukahlukene ezikhethekile, amagama noma imisho okufanele yeqiwe.
Uma uchofoza inkinobho yokulanda, ungalanda izinqwaba zezihlungi engizazidalile.
Ungangeza futhi owakho uma kunesidingo.
[Umsebenzi wokuvala okuzenzakalelayo] Ikuvumela ukuthi usethe isikhathi esizenzakalelayo sokuvala ngokuzenzakalelayo ngokuthinta isithonjana esenziwe ngenyanga ngakwesobunxele inkinobho yokumisa lapho udlala ama-TTS.


8. Incazelo yokusekelayo ne-Revation Function:
[Izilungiselelo - Isipele nokubuyiselwa] kungumsebenzi owenzelwe ukukuvumela ukuthi usebenzise uhlelo lokusebenza endaweni efanayo kumadivayisi amaningi.
Ungangena kwi-drive bese ubuyisela ama-backups.
Lapho ubukwa kumadivayisi amaningi afana ne-PC, ifoni, noma i-pad, ungavumelanisa indawo yokubuka usebenzisa isipele sendawo.
Uma ukhetha idivaysi ukuvumelanisa ngokuzenzakalela bese uvula umsebenzi we- [Moto Sync], izohamba ngokuzenzakalelayo endaweni yokugcina ebukelwe lapho uqala uhlelo lokusebenza noma ushiya bese ubuyela ekhasini lasekhaya.

9. Ungawusebenzisa kanjani umbhalo wokuhlela we-batch:
1. [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo - Khombisa inkinobho ye-Batch Hlela]
2. [Izilungiselelo - Umbhalo Wokuhlela Enqwaba]
Ukuhlelwa kwenqwaba kungasetshenziswa ngezindlela ezimbili.
Uma kwenzeka u-1, uma imisho ethile noma amagama ecasulayo ngenkathi ulalele ama-TTS, kusetshenziselwa ukusula imisho noma ukuguqula kwabanye izinhlamvu.
Uma kwenzeka 2, ungakhipha umbhalo usebenzisa i-OCR bese ukhetha amafayela wombhalo kusuka kumtapo wezincwadi wami ukwenza ukuhlelwa kwe-batch.
Ungasebenzisa futhi inkinobho yokulanda ku-batch Hlela Umbhalo ukuze ulande amaphutha avela njalo.
[I-Batch Hlela - Landa] izokhombisa kuphela uhlu uma ukulungiswa kolimi kunguKorea.
Lokhu kungenxa yokuthi abathuthukisi abazi ukuthi bazikhulume kanjani ezinye izilimi.
Uma ukubika usebenzisa umsebenzi wombiko, sizokwengeza ulimi lwezwe lakho.

10. Isixazululo uma kunomusho ocasulayo lapho ulalele ama-TTS:
Uma ungenandaba nokuguqulwa kwasekuqaleni, sicela usebenzise i- [Batch Hlela] umsebenzi ukuze uyiguqule.
Ngemuva kwezinguquko, ifayela eliyisipele lizosala umtapo wezincwadi wami, ngakho-ke uma wenza izinguquko ngephutha, ungababuyisela.
Uma ufuna ukususa ama-TTS kuphela ngaphandle kokuthinta okwangempela, sebenzisa isihlungi se-TTS.

11. Ukuchazwa okuhlobene namaphoyinti:
Amaphoyinti adliwa lapho usebenzisa imisebenzi ekhethekile efana nokuhlolwa kwesigwebo, ukuhlela umugqa, ukuhlelwa komugqa, kanye nokuhumusha, kepha njengoba zingaba ngu-10 kuye ku-50, ngeke uzizwe ukusetshenziswa.
Amanani amaningi kungenzeka ukuthi asetshenziswe ngezinga elithile elingazwakala lapho ekhipha umbhalo kusuka ku-OCR, ePub, ne-PDF.
Lapho sikhipha umbhalo, asikhiphi nje umbhalo, kepha futhi dala amaphethini avela ezinkingeni eziningi eziphakamayo ngemuva kokukhishwa futhi ziqhubeke nenhlangano yemisho.
Le ngxenye yenziwa ngobunzima obukhulu, ngakho-ke ngicela uzame ukuyisebenzisa okungenani kanye.


12. Ukukhangisa okuhlobene nokukhangisa:
Ngingunjiniyela nonjiniyela nonjiniyela.
Izikhangiso zingeziwe kuze kufike lapho zingaphazamisi ukusetshenziswa ngangokunokwenzeka.
Kunezinhlobo ezimbili zezikhangiso: Amaphuzu athola izikhangiso kanye nezikhangiso zokususa zokukhangisa.
Ungabamba amaphuzu afinyelela kuma-60,000.
Lapho ususa izikhangiso, ungasebenzisa amaphuzu ukususa izikhangiso isikhathi esithile. (Ngaphandle kwesikhathi sokulala, cishe usuku olulodwa.)
Kulabo aqoqa amaphuzu kusenesikhathi, ungasusa izikhangiso eziseceleni kwesibukeli ngokuphuza amaphuzu angama-2000.
Uma uqeda amaphuzu angama-8000, zonke izikhangiso zizokhutshazwa ngaphandle kokukhangiswa kwama-30 kwesibili ukuze kuvikeleke amaphuzu kanye nomkhangiso wendabuko phakathi kwezilungiselelo.
Ukukhangiswa kwamaphuzu angama-8000 kwengezwa kwalabo abakuthola kuyacasula ukubona izikhangiso ezimfushane eziphuma lapho wenza imisebenzi enkulu yokuguqula.


13. Incazelo yokuqhubeka (umlando wokuxhumana ngomculo):
Isekela umsebenzi wokubuka okuqhubekayo.
Isibonelo, ake sithi kukhona idokhumenti enesihloko esithi (1 ~ 50), kepha manje usuthola idokhumenti entsha enesihloko esithi (1 ~ 100).
Kulokhu, usuvele usuvele weqe indawo eyinhloko.
Uma ukhetha ifayela lombhalo kulabhulali yami, imenyu yokuqhubeka iyavela.
Ngemuva kokukhetha le menyu, uma ukhetha (1 ~ 50) Emlandweni, kuzokweqa indawo ebhekwe ngaphambilini.
Uma usunaso isikhathi emuva kwesikhathi, sicela ususe (1 ~ 50) emlandweni.
Emlandweni, inani eliphelele lezincwadi ezifundwe libalwa.
Uma idokhumenti efanayo ihlala, imininingwane engalungile ingakhonjiswa.


14. I-bookmark / memo / star rating ncazelo:
Uhlelo lokusebenza lisekela imemo kanye ne-bookmark / star acting imisebenzi.
Uma uvula umsebenzi we-bookmark kuzilungiselelo ze-bookmark, ungangeza ngokucindezela isikhathi eside umbhalo.
Umsebenzi we-Memo ukuvumela ukuthi ukhethe futhi ufake ifayela uma uvule futhi ubuke ifayela okungenani kanye.
Uma uvula ifayela ngisho kanye, kunenkanyezi phezulu lapho ukhetha ifayela.
Amanothi nezilinganiso zingasetjenziselwa ukurekhoda ukuhlolwa kwefayela uqobo.
Amabhukhimakhi angasetshenziselwa ukuqopha imininingwane ebalulekile.



14. Incazelo yokuhlola umusho:
Lapho ngibheka amadokhumenti amaningi, ngaqala ukubona amaphethini phakathi kwezihloko ezibhalwe kahle nezingezincwadi ezinjalo.
Ngithole iphethini ehlala ivela ezihlokweni ezidumazayo.
Lona ngumsebenzi engidalile ngokuhlela la maphethini futhi enikeza isisindo kwiphethini ngayinye.
Ekuqaleni, lo msebenzi waqanjwa ngokuthi [Minesweeper].
Kodwa-ke, lo msebenzi wadalwa ngokusekelwe esahlulekelweni esinqunyiwe kakhulu, ngakho-ke kwaguqulwa kwaba yi-TTS Checker.
Lesi sici sisekela kuphela isiKorea.
Ngoba angazi ezinye izilimi zamazwe kahle, angikwazanga ukuthola ukuthi ukubhala kwakukuhle noma kubi.
Uma kunephethini elinjengale kwamanye amazwe, sicela ulibike nge-imeyili.
Lapho nje singasekela izilimi eziningi zezwe, sizohambisa isici kuzilungiselelo.
Kusekhona isikhala esisodwa esisele kuzilungiselelo.


15. Ukuchazwa komsebenzi we-Line Break:
I-Line Break Theorem iyindlela engasetshenziswa lapho umusho ungaqediwe kepha ukhiye wokungena ucindezelwa.
Le nto ivame ukwenzeka kumadokhumenti acutshungulwe ngokuguqulwa kwe-OCR.
Lo msebenzi awuhlanganisi nje ukungena.
Lo msebenzi wasetshenziswa ngokuhlaziya amadokhumenti anamaphutha amaningi amasha kanye nokuhlela amaphethini.
Ngazama ukwethula ukuhlela ngisebenzisa i-AI, kepha kwakungelula.
Lezi zinsuku, ucwaningo oluningi lwenziwa ekucutshungweni kolimi lwemvelo, ngakho-ke uma singalucubungula nge-AI ngokuzayo, sizozama ukungeza.

16. Incazelo yomsebenzi wokuhumusha:
Usekela imisebenzi yokuhumusha yendawo yokuhumusha.
Ukuhumusha kwendawo kungumsebenzi ohumusha usebenzisa i-AI athuthukiswe yi-Google. Amagama alula ahunyushelwe kahle, kepha kubonakala sengathi akhona ukungahambi kahle okumbalwa ekuhumusheni umusho.
Ukuhunyushwa kwesiphequluli seWebhu kuwumsebenzi obonisa umusho owuhunyushwe esipheqululini.
Uma uvula umsebenzi wokuhumusha ku [Izilungiselelo - Izilungiselelo Zokuhumusha] bese ucindezela bese ubamba umusho, umsebenzi wokuhumusha uzosebenza.

17. Ezinye izindaba:
Lapho uvula idokhumenti, i-EarApp izoshintsha ngokuzenzakalelayo ukhiye wokufaka ophindaphindwe kathathu noma ngaphezulu amahlandla amabili kabili.
Ukungena ngokweqile akukhululeki ukulalela nokubuka nge-TTS.
Abanye kini kungenzeka ukuthi sebevele bakubonile lokhu ngenkathi besebenzisa uhlelo lwami lokusebenza isikhathi eside, bese uthi, "Hmm? Manje njengoba ngicabanga ngakho, angikaze ngikubone ucindezela u-Enter okuningi? " Abanye abantu bangacabanga kanjalo.
Sisebenzise izici eziningi ngangokunokwenzeka esibukweni sombhalo.

Ngabhala phansi izindlela eziyishumi nesikhombisa zokuyisebenzisa, kepha angicabangi ukuthi ngibhale nengxenye yalo.
Imisebenzi elula efana nombala wangemuva, isithombe sangemuva, umbala wombhalo, kanye nendlela yokuguqula iphuzu kushiyiwe.
Ngiyethemba ukuthi le ndatshana izokusiza kubantu abaningi.
Uma ubheka [izilungiselelo - Ulwazi nosizo], kunesixhumanisi segumbi lengxoxo yeTelegram Group, ngakho-ke uma unemibuzo, ungangena ubuze.
Ngiyabonga ngokufunda.
